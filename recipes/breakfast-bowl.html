Breakfast Bowl

I eat this almost every morning for breakfast. It's fast, easy to make, and nutritious

Ingredients

  • One apple
  • Granola
  • Homemade yogurt

Steps

  1. Cut apple into small cubes.
  2. Add one or two dollops of homemade yogurt.
  3. Sprinkle granola on top.
  4. Mix and enjoy.
