Salad

I eat this frequently for lunch or dinner. It's tasty and very healthy.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • One large carrot
  • One side of red bell pepper
  • Black olives
  • Baby corn
  • Cheese
  • Homemade salad dressing

Steps

  1. Clean and slice vegetables and add to a large bowl
  2. Sprinkel grated cheese over the top
  3. Add homemade salad dressing and mix
